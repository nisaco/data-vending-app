Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: November 11, 2025**

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to process your data purchases and maintain your account security.

  • **Personal Data:** Username, Email address, and Password (stored securely as a hash).
  • **Transaction Data:** Recipient phone number, Network, Data plan amount, and Payment reference (Paystack).
  • **Session Data:** We use cookies and MongoDB session storage to keep you logged in and to monitor active user status for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill data orders (e.g., sharing your phone number with the network API).
  • To maintain and update your digital wallet balance.
  • For system administration and security (e.g., preventing fraud and monitoring login attempts).

3. Data Security and Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share only necessary transaction details (recipient phone, network, data plan ID) with the third-party network reseller API to fulfill your order. Your payment information (card numbers, MoMo PINs) is handled entirely by Paystack and is never stored on our servers.

For questions regarding this policy, please contact us via the Support page.
